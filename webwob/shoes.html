Smarter Shoe Care: Anti-Bacterial Drying System with PID Control

Combining Controlled Heating and Ozone Sterilization for Better Hygiene 👟

Wet shoes aren’t just uncomfortable—they can become a breeding ground for odor and bacteria. This project introduces a smart drying system that combines controlled heating and ozone sterilization to deliver fast, safe, and hygienic drying.

🌡️ Controlled Drying Meets Hygiene

The system uses a closed-loop control approach to maintain optimal drying conditions:

  • Controlled heat removes moisture efficiently
  • Ozone generator eliminates bacteria and odor
  • Continuous temperature monitoring ensures safety

This results in faster drying, improved hygiene, and better protection of shoe materials.

🧠 Why PID Control?

The system uses a PID (Proportional–Integral–Derivative) controller to regulate temperature precisely.

  • Continuously adjusts heating output
  • Maintains stable target temperature
  • Prevents overheating and material damage

This ensures efficient drying while preserving the integrity of the shoes.

🔌 Electrical Design & Wiring
